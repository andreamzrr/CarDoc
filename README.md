CarDoc is a free, all-in-one car care platform built for everyday drivers who just want to get from point A to point B without the stress of unexpected repairs or costly mechanic visits. Accessible on any device, CarDoc gives you everything you need to understand, maintain, and repair your vehicle  all in one place!
Using our intuitive diagnosis tool, simply describe your issue and get clear, reliable guidance in seconds. Need to look something up? Our simple AI search feature puts thousands of car issues and solutions at your fingertips. For those who want to stay ahead of problems, our maintenance timeline keeps you organized with scheduled reminders and service history at a glance.
CarDoc also features a personal Garage where you can store all your vehicles, log maintenance records, and track important details over time. Going deeper, our step by step repair guides walk you through fixes in plain language, no mechanical experience required. You can even scan your VIN to instantly pull up your vehicle's full profile and check for any active recalls that could affect your safety.
And when a job is too big to tackle alone, our recommended mechanics feature connects you with trusted, nearby professionals so you always know who to call.
